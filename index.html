About Me

My interest in mehndi design began in childhood. When I was 15, I decided to apply mehndi on my own hand one day. A neighbor saw the design, admired it, and asked if I could apply mehndi on her hands as well. I eagerly agreed, and after completing the design, she complimented me, which sparked my passion for mehndi. From then on, I started applying mehndi for family and friends on various occasions. My confidence grew significantly when I received an order to design mehndi for a bride, who was very pleased with my work. This experience deepened my appreciation for the art of mehndi. Guided by my commitment to excellence, I am passionate about providing exceptional mehndi services that highlight the beauty and artistry of this timeless tradition. Let’s craft something special together!

~ Tejaswani Venkat

Start Learing

Embarking on the journey to learn mehndi is an exciting and rewarding experience that welcomes individuals of all skill levels. Whether you’re drawn to the intricate patterns or the cultural significance of this beautiful art form, starting to learn mehndi is both accessible and enjoyable. With practice and a bit of guidance, anyone can master the techniques and create stunning designs. From simple patterns to elaborate creations, the art of mehndi offers endless possibilities for personal expression and creativity. Dive into the world of mehndi, and discover how this timeless tradition can be a source of joy and accomplishment in your life.
